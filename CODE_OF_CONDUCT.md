# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want MotionForge 3D to be a professional, respectful, and evidence-driven open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and make room for people with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and project goals.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Avoid harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Be careful with sensitive data, especially private video, health-adjacent context, credentials, and personally identifiable information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the GitHub profile. Do not include private media, credentials, or unrelated personal information in public issues.
